# Quadratic Formula

$$x=\frac{-b\pm\sqrt{b^2-4ac}}{2a}$$

## Substitutions

1. Write the standard equation: $$2x^2-5x+1=0$$
1. Find a, b, c: $$a=2, b=-5, c=1$$
1. Apply:
$$x=\frac{-(-5)\pm\sqrt{(-5)^2-4(2)(1)}}{2(2)}$$
$$x=\frac{5\pm\sqrt{25-8}}{4}$$
$$x=\frac{5\pm\sqrt{17}}{4}$$
